\section{'Neural Network Learning of Black-Scholes Equation for Option Pricing' by Daniel De Souza Santos and Tiago Alessandro Espinola Ferreira'}

This paper presents an approach to use neural network learning to solve the Black-Scholes equations. 
Time series data of call option prices of Brazilian companies Petrobras and Vale have been used.
